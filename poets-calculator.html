    About POETS

    POETS was developed from a bicentric retrospective cohort of 9,062 adults undergoing primary hip, knee, or shoulder arthroplasty.

    The score predicts early transfusion — defined as any red blood cell transfusion administered between hospital admission and discharge from the post-anaesthesia care unit (PACU). This is the only window during which preoperative type-and-screen availability is clinically decisive.

    A score below 5 identifies patients at very low risk of early transfusion in whom routine preoperative type-and-screen testing may be safely omitted. Postoperative transfusions on the ward are managed with standard biological monitoring.
